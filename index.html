While Unemployment Rates Drop, Black Unemployment Remains High

The economy has been on an upward swing post pandemic, why aren't Black workers seeing the results

--December 14, 2023--

By: Marissa Braswell

Since President Biden has taken office he has been pushing his plan called “Build Back Better”. The plan aims to build America’s Middle Class after years of shrinking.

Since then Biden has been lauding his achievements with this plan for helping the economy bounce back post-pandemic. These achievements include lowering inflation, adding millions of jobs, and low unemployment rates.

In 2023, the overall unemployment rate has stayed below 4% for the entire year. This is the longest time a rate has been that low in 50 years. However, the unemployment rate of Black workers has remained over 4% all year with most months over 5%.
